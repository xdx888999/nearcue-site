快速上手

  1. 在 Mac 上安装并打开 NearCue(菜单栏图标),点击「显示配对码」。
  2. 在 iPhone 上打开 NearCue,选择发现的 Mac,输入六位配对码,并在 Mac 上批准。
  3. 在 Mac 的 NearCue 设置中「启用控制」,按系统提示授予辅助功能权限。
  4. 把想控制的应用(Codex、Claude、终端等)切到前台,点击「添加应用」批准。
  5. 现在就能用 iPhone 或 Apple Watch 确认、停止、语音输入了。

常见问题

iPhone 找不到我的 Mac?
确认两台设备在同一 Wi-Fi(或蓝牙可达范围内),且首次启动时已允许 NearCue 使用「本地网络」。可在 iPhone 设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 本地网络 中检查。
按了按钮但 Mac 没反应?
检查三件事:① Mac 端已授予辅助功能权限;② 要控制的应用在 Mac 前台;③ 该应用已在 NearCue 中批准。状态卡会显示当前正在控制的应用。
Apple Watch 上怎么安装?
无需单独安装:装好 iPhone 版后,手表端会自动出现(或在 Watch App 中手动安装)。手表经 iPhone 中继与 Mac 通信。
语音听写的文字会自动发送吗?
不会。听写文字只插入 Mac 当前输入框,永远不会自动确认或提交——最后一步始终由你决定。
免费版和正式版的区别?
免费版:配对一台 Mac,使用确认、停止与 Mac 语音开关。一次性买断解锁全部功能(听写、全部按键、命令库、手表快捷键、多 Mac),iPhone、Apple Watch、Mac 三端通用,无订阅。
换了手机或重装后如何恢复购买?
在 iPhone 的 NearCue 设置或购买页点「恢复购买」,使用购买时的 Apple 账户即可。
我的代码和数据安全吗?
NearCue 只在本地网络通信,不经互联网;不读取屏幕、代码或剪贴板;无账号无云端。详见隐私政策。

Getting started

  1. Install and open NearCue on your Mac (menu bar icon), then click "Show Pairing Code".
  2. On iPhone, pick the discovered Mac, enter the six-digit code, and approve on the Mac.
  3. Enable control in the Mac app and grant the Accessibility permission.
  4. Bring the app you want to control (Codex, Claude, a terminal…) to the front and approve it via "Add App".
  5. Confirm, stop, and dictate from iPhone or Apple Watch.

FAQ

iPhone can't find my Mac?
Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi (or within Bluetooth range) and that NearCue has Local Network permission on iPhone (Settings → Privacy & Security → Local Network).
Buttons don't do anything on the Mac?
Check that ① Accessibility permission is granted on the Mac, ② the target app is frontmost, and ③ the app is approved in NearCue.
How do I install the Apple Watch app?
No separate install: it ships inside the iPhone app and appears on your watch automatically (or install it from the Watch app).
Is dictated text submitted automatically?
Never. Dictation only inserts text into the current Mac input. You always make the final call.
Free vs. full version?
Free: pair one Mac, with Confirm, Stop, and the Mac voice toggle. A one-time purchase unlocks everything across iPhone, Apple Watch, and Mac. No subscription.
How do I restore my purchase?
Tap "Restore Purchases" in NearCue's settings or purchase screen, signed in with the Apple account used for the purchase.
