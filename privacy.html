隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 22 日

NearCue 是一款本地优先的遥控器应用，让 iPhone 与 Apple Watch 在附近本地网络内遥控你自己 Mac 上的 AI 编程工具。我们不收集、不存储你的任何个人数据。

1. 我们不收集数据

  • NearCue 没有账号系统、没有云端服务、没有行为分析，也不包含任何第三方数据收集 SDK。
  • 我们没有用于处理遥控数据的服务器；应用之间只在你的本地网络内直接通信。

2. 本地网络通信

  • iPhone、Apple Watch 与 Mac 之间的遥控指令只经附近本地网络传输，必要时使用点对点链路，全程加密，不经过我们的服务器。
  • 每台 Mac 都需要你输入六位配对码并在 Mac 上明确批准后才能被控制。

3. 语音与麦克风

  • Mac 收音模式：NearCue 只远程触发你在 Mac 上自行配置的语音工具，NearCue 本身不采集音频。
  • iPhone 听写仅在你主动操作时使用麦克风，并使用设备端语音识别；Apple Watch 使用系统听写界面。
  • 两种方式都只把识别出的文字发送到你已配对的 Mac，NearCue 不保存音频。

4. 我们不读取你的内容

  • NearCue 不读取 Mac 的屏幕、窗口文字、终端输出、代码或剪贴板，只执行你主动触发的动作并返回成功或失败回执。
  • 每个受控应用都需要你在 Mac 上手动批准。

5. 本地存储

  • 配对信任密钥保存在系统钥匙串（Keychain），控制配置保存在设备本地。
  • 诊断信息经过脱敏，且只能由你手动导出。

6. 购买

应用内购买完全由 Apple 的 App Store 处理，我们无法接触你的支付信息。Mac 接收端可从 NearCue 官网免费下载。

7. 儿童隐私

NearCue 不收集任何人的数据，也不收集儿童数据。

8. 政策变更与联系方式

如本政策发生变更，我们会在此页面更新。任何疑问请联系：xdx_lab@126.com

English

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

NearCue is a local-first remote that lets your iPhone and Apple Watch control AI coding tools on your own Mac over the nearby local network. We do not collect or store any of your personal data.

1. No data collection

  • NearCue has no accounts, no cloud services, no analytics, and no third-party data-collection SDKs.
  • We operate no server for remote-control traffic. The apps communicate directly on your local network.

2. Local network communication

  • Remote commands travel only over the nearby local network, peer-to-peer when needed, and are encrypted. They never pass through our servers.
  • Every Mac must be explicitly paired with a six-digit code and approved on the Mac itself.

3. Voice and microphone

  • Mac voice mode only triggers the voice tool you configured on your Mac; NearCue itself captures no audio.
  • iPhone dictation uses the microphone only when you actively choose it and uses on-device speech recognition; Apple Watch uses the system dictation interface.
  • Only recognized text is sent to your paired Mac. NearCue does not store audio.

4. We never read your content

  • NearCue does not read your Mac screen, window text, terminal output, code, or clipboard. It only performs actions you trigger and returns a success or failure receipt.
  • Every controlled application must be manually approved on the Mac.

5. Local storage

  • Pairing trust keys are stored in the system Keychain; settings stay on your devices.
  • Diagnostics are redacted and can only be exported manually by you.

6. Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We never see your payment information. The Mac receiver is available as a free download from the NearCue website.

7. Children's privacy

NearCue collects no data from anyone, including children.

8. Changes and contact

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page. Questions: xdx_lab@126.com
